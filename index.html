Site Details:

  • Grailed - Shoes, clothes, purses and accessories all by high end designers. Considered to be the place to find things you can't find anywhere else
  • StockX - ALL things shoes! The leading shoes site but is now starting to include clothes, purses and other collectibles
  • G.O.A.T. - Also focused primarily on shoes and also guarantees the authenticity of every purchase or your money back
  • Chrono24 - Considered to to be THE authority on watches, from Rolex and Patek Philippe to Breitling and Cartier
  • Jomashop - Started as a watch site but now inlcudes designer sunglasses and a growing amount of apparel as well
  • Pen Boutique - The place to find any and every pen you can imagine. The leading seller of currently available and collectible retired Montblanc pens
